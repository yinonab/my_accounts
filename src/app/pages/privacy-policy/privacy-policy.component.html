Developer Policies

To maintain a high standard of quality and integrity across our platform, we expect developers to adhere to the following policies when using our Social Technologies Developer Center. These policies ensure that user experiences remain positive and secure while fostering responsible and ethical development practices.

1. Prohibited Practices

Developers must not engage in activities that undermine user trust, platform security, or community integrity. This includes:

  • Hosting, promoting, or facilitating the distribution of malicious software or code.
  • Managing applications using inauthentic or fake accounts, including bots and unauthorized automation tools.
  • Engaging in activities that violate user privacy or exploit platform vulnerabilities.

2. Transparency and Ad-Buying Solutions

Developers are required to uphold transparency standards, particularly when handling advertising solutions. This includes:

  • Clearly disclosing the purpose, intent, and usage of data collected from users.
  • Ensuring compliance with all applicable laws and platform requirements regarding advertising transparency and disclosures.
  • Adhering to ethical practices in ad management, including avoiding misleading or deceptive content.

3. App Development Guidelines

To support the creation of high-quality applications, we provide the following guidelines:

  • Quality Controls: Implement thorough testing to prevent bugs, crashes, and performance issues.
  • User Experience: Prioritize intuitive navigation, accessibility, and seamless functionality.
  • Content Requirements: Ensure all content aligns with community guidelines and does not promote harmful, offensive, or misleading material.
  • Functional Standards: Avoid excessive permissions requests and provide clear explanations for required permissions.

4. Policy Updates and Readability

To simplify compliance and enhance clarity:

  • Outdated or redundant policies have been removed.
  • Specific sections have been revised to clearly define their scope and applicability.
  • Developers are encouraged to review the updated guidelines regularly to remain informed about any changes.

Conclusion

By adhering to these policies, developers contribute to a safe, secure, and enriching ecosystem for all users. Non-compliance may result in penalties, including suspension or removal of access to the Developer Center.

We value your contributions and are committed to supporting your success within our community.
